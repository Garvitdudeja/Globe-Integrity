Complementary Insurance Plans

See our suggestions for insurance plans that work well together. All of these complementary plans can be purchased as standalone products or paired with others and are underwritten by Golden Rule Insurance Company.

TriTerm Medical
Triterm Medical Insurance offers comprehensive coverage tailored for short-to-medium term health needs. This policy provides essential protection against unexpected medical expenses, including hospital visits, treatments, and specialist care. Designed for individuals seeking flexible, reliable coverage for a defined period, Triterm Medical Insurance ensures peace of mind with a focus on affordability and extensive benefits
Critical Illness
Critical Illness Insurance provides financial protection by offering a lump-sum payout if you are diagnosed with a covered serious illness, such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. This coverage helps cover medical expenses, lost income, and other costs associated with recovery, allowing you to focus on your health without the added stress of financial burdens.
Dental
Dental Insurance helps cover the cost of routine dental care, including cleanings, exams, fillings, and more extensive procedures like crowns and root canals. With coverage designed to support both preventive and restorative treatments, this plan ensures that you can maintain optimal oral health while managing your expenses effectively.
Vision
Vision Insurance provides coverage for essential eye care services, including eye exams, glasses, and contact lenses. It also helps with costs related to corrective procedures and treatments for various vision conditions. Designed to support your eye health and improve visual clarity, this insurance plan makes routine eye care more affordable and accessible
Hospital Indemnity
Hospital Indemnity Insurance offers financial support by providing a fixed cash benefit for each day you are hospitalized due to illness or injury. This coverage helps offset out-of-pocket expenses such as deductibles, copayments, and daily living costs, allowing you to focus on your recovery without worrying about the financial impact.
Term
Term Accident Safeguard Insurance provides financial protection in the event of an accidental injury or death. This policy offers a lump-sum benefit if you suffer a serious accident or pass away due to an accident during the policy term. Designed to offer peace of mind, it helps cover medical expenses, lost income, and other costs associated with accidental injuries or fatalities.
